Matar Panner

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • fried Paneer
  • 1 Bayleaf
  • 2-3 cloves and 1 cardamom
  • 1 tsp Cumin seed
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped
  • 1 fresh Green chilli, chopped
  • 2 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • ½ tsp Turmeric powder
  • 11/2 tsp Degi red chilli powder
  • 2 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1½ cups fresh Tomato puree
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup Green peas

Instruction

  1. In a kadai heat ghee and add cloves, cumin seeds let them crackle then add onion and saute until translucent.
  2. Now add green chilli, ginger-garlic paste, and saute for a minute.
  3. Then add turmeric powder, degi red chilli powder, coriander powder and saute until the onions are properly cooked and spices are fragrant.
  4. At this time add the fresh tomato puree, salt and saute until it’s cooked or the ghee starts coming from sides.
  5. Then add the green peas, fried paneer without water and saute for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Now add some water and boil it for 5-6 minutes until a semi thick gravy.
  7. switch off the flames and serve hot in a serving bowl and garnish with coriander leaves.
